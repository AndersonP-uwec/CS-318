Her Appearance

She is a brown-grey tabby. She is almost two years old. She has green eyes. Her fur is very soft. She is very photogenic and she knows it.

She likes to bite and is very playful. She likes to steal human food but not to eat it. Her favorite toys are her little stuffed animals. She is affectionate when she isn't playful or angry.
